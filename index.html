About Me

Artist Portrait

Professional Makeup Artist

Hello! I'm a passionate and creative makeup artist with over 8 years of experience in the beauty industry. My journey began with a fascination for colors and textures, which naturally evolved into a love for the transformative power of makeup.

I specialize in creating looks that enhance natural beauty while achieving the perfect balance between timeless elegance and current trends. Whether it's bridal makeup that needs to last all day, editorial looks that make a statement, or everyday beauty that boosts confidence, I approach each client with personalized attention and care.

My philosophy is simple: makeup should make you feel like the best version of yourself. I believe in using high-quality products that not only look stunning but also take care of your skin.

I've had the privilege of working with clients from diverse backgrounds for weddings, photoshoots, special events, and one-on-one makeup lessons. Each experience has enriched my skills and deepened my appreciation for this art form.
